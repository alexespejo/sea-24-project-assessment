A Critics Collection of the Greatest Albums of All Time

More About This Page

This web page is a collection of albums sourced by music critics on the website Album of the Year (AOTY).

This site features a minimalistic and contemporary museum-inspired design intended to draw the eye to the iconic album covers and titles of artists, rappers, songwriters, and musicians who have revolutionized the sound of modern music.

From Michael Jackson's Thriller to Pink Floyd's The Dark Side of the Moon, placing these albums in this museum of music is a way to venerate these artists and their work for the long-lasting impact they will have on the art.
